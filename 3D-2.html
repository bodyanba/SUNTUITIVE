Suntuitive mitigates glare caused by the low morning sun and will automatically return to clear as the sun moves over the building

With it's neutral appearance Suntuitive can be combined with combined with conventional glass on the same building in areas that do not receive sunshine, making for a very cost-effective solution

Suntuitive is the only Dynamic Glass with built-in intelligence, that only tints the glass where needed and stays clear in areas that are shaded, by trees for example

Blocking up to 90% of solar energy, Suntuitive allows architects to design large areas of glass without having to worry about prohibitive heat load penalties while maximizing occupant comfort
